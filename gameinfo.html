Everything You Need to Know About PulseStrike

What is PulseStrike?

PulseStrike is a modern tactical first-person shooter inspired by the intensity of games like Call of Duty: Modern Warfare. It blends fast-paced gunfights with strategic decision-making, specialist Operators, and dynamic environments.

Built for both casual and competitive players, PulseStrike brings you a cinematic military experience with immersive visuals, realistic combat physics, and a storyline that drops you into the heart of a global conflict.

Core Gameplay Features

Realistic Gunplay

High fidelity ballistics, recoil patterns, and weapon mastery system.

Dynamic Operators

Each Operator has unique abilities, gadgets, and tactical roles.

Destructible Environments

Walls, doors, and cover react to explosions and gunfire.
